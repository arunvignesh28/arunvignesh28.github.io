Behind the Lens

The eye that finds stories in still frames

Photography has been my creative counterpart to research. Where science demands precision, photography rewards patience — the willingness to wait for the light, the angle, the fleeting expression that transforms an ordinary scene into something worth remembering.

My style leans towards cinematic depth and natural light. I love high-contrast environments — golden hour landscapes, gritty street scenes, intimate portraits that feel unposed. Every frame is a puzzle of composition, light, and timing.

Based in Tempe, AZ. Available for sessions across the Phoenix metro area and for travel engagements.
